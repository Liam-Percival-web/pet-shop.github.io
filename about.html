About our Pretty Paws Pet Shop

Our About page shares the story behind Pretty Paws Pet Shop and the passion that drives us. What began as a love for cats has grown into a welcoming space for pets of every kind. We’re committed to providing a warm, knowledgeable service and stocking products we’d happily use for our own animals. Our goal is simple: to support pet owners and celebrate the joy that pets bring to everyday life.


Where we are located

We’re proudly based in the heart of Skelmersdale, where our team works every day to support our community and the pets they love. Our shop is easy to reach, and we’re always happy to welcome visitors who want advice, supplies, or just a friendly chat about their furry companions. Whether you’re nearby or travelling in from surrounding areas, you’ll always find a warm welcome waiting for you.
